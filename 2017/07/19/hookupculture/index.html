The hookup culture

 source: [American Psychological Association](http://www.apa.org/monitor/2013/02/ce-corner.aspx)

Translated by： Catherine Wei

“Hookups,” or uncommitted sexual encounters, are becoming progressively more engrained in popular
culture, reflecting both evolved sexual predilections and changing social and sexual scripts.

“约炮，” 或不受约束的性接触，植根于大众文化，反映了发展的性爱好，以及改变的社会和性的脚本。

什么是约炮（Hookup）？

Hook-up activities
may include a wide range of sexual behaviors, such as kissing, oral sex, and penetrative intercourse. However,
these encounters often transpire without any promise of, or desire for, a more traditional romantic relationship.

约炮包括包括一个广泛的性行为领域，例如亲吻、口交和性交。但是，这些邂逅常常出现在没有任何承诺，或渴望一个传统的浪漫关系（的前提上）。

生活中与约炮（Hookup）相关的

Popular media representations of sexuality demonstrate the pervasiveness of a sexual hookup culture among emerging adults. The themes of books, plots of movies and television shows, and lyrics of numerous songs all demonstrate a permissive sexuality among consumers.

大众媒体无处不在地在表现兴起的成年人性文化（约炮）。 书的主题，电影和电视节目情节，无数歌曲的歌词都表现出在消费者中放纵的性行为。

As an example, the lyrics above, from the chart-topping
pop song Last Friday Night (T.G.I.F.) by singersongwriter
Katy Perry highlight someone’s Friday night partying, presumably including casual sex, alcohol, and a piecemeal memory of the nights events.

举一个例子，在流行歌曲排行榜唱作人 Katy Perry的 “Last Friday Night” (T.G.I.F.)的歌词中强调了某人周五晚上的派对，大致包括性行为，酒精和关于夜晚的零碎记忆。

流行文化中的 Hookup

Popular media, including television, has become a source of sex education, filled with (inaccurate) portrayals of sexuality (Kunkel et al., 2005; Strasburger, 2005; Ward, 2003)

大众媒体，包括电视，充满（不准确的）性形象已成为了性教育的源头。

Many popular representations suggest uncommitted sex, or
hookups, can be both biophysically and emotionally enjoyable and occur without “strings.”

许多流行的描述暗示了未表态的性行为（约炮）可以是生理和情感上的愉悦并且发生在没有“牵线“的情况下。

Popular television shows often portray
hooking up as acceptable, entertaining, and perfectly sensible.

流行的电视剧常常描述约炮为受欢迎的，使人愉快的并且完全合乎情理的事。

The film “No Strings Attached”, released in 2011 and staring Natalie Portman and Ashton Kutcher, features the uncommitted element of uncommitted sex, as two friends attempt to negotiate a sexual, yet nonromantic, component of their relationship.

2011年的电影“No Strings Attached” 突出了未表态这一元素的约炮行为，两个朋友试着谈论出一个性，但非浪漫的要素来称为他们关系的水准。

朋友的炮友关系（Friends With Benefits）

In terms of popular public discourse, Urban Dictionary defines FWBs as “two friends who have a sexual relationship without being emotionally involved. Typically two good friends who have casual sex without a monogamous relationship or any kind of commitment” (Friends with benefits, 2003)

大众论述是这样的，Urban Dictionary 定义 FWBS（friends with benefits）为两个有性关系，但没有感情关系的朋友。通常是两个没有一夫一妻制关系或没有任何承诺的性交行为的活动。

“a safe relationship, that mimics a real partnership but is void or greatly lacking jealousy and other such emotions that come with a serious relationship” (Friends with benefits, 2005).

“一种安全的关系，模仿一种真正的伴侣关系，但是没有或极度缺乏嫉妒及其他与严肃关系产生的感情”。
