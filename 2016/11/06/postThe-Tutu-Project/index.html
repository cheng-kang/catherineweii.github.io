The Tutu Project

发表于 2016-11-06   |  

Source
Translate: Catherine Wei

Cancer is a thing no one would ever dare laugh about. However, with all the difficulties surrounding the illness, laughter can be the best medicine. The greatest example of this approach is The Tutu Project, a fun and simply endearing effort of a man who wanted to make things easier and funnier for his wife after she was diagnosed with breast cancer.

癌症是一件没有人敢笑的事。但无论如何，面对所有疾病周围的困难，笑声可以是最好的药物。实现这种方法的例子是蓬蓬裙项目（The Tutu Project），在他的妻子被诊断出乳腺癌时，他想让她更加幸福，开心。因此，他花费精力去做了一个有趣而简单，讨人喜欢的项目。

A man with a pink tutu might be all you need when things get dark. This is what photographer Bob Carey decided to do when he found out about his wife Linda’s diagnosis of breast cancer – he donned the pink ballet accessory and took photos of himself in various different and unexpected places. All this just to make his wife laugh.

当你在黑暗时光里时，一个穿着粉色蓬蓬裙的男人可能是你全部的需要了。这是当摄影师 Bob Carey 得知他的妻子 Linda 被诊断为乳腺癌时决定去做的事情 - 他穿上粉色的芭蕾装饰品并记录了他自己在许多不同和出乎意料的地方拍的照片。所有的一切只是为了逗他的妻子笑。

##Bob and Linda Carey

##Linda was diagnosed with breast cancer in 2003,
Linda 在2003年被诊断为乳腺癌

The only thing Bob could think of is to make her laugh and innocently happy for a moment or a day, so Bob put on a pink tutu

Bob唯一能想到的就是让Linda能有个天真快乐的时刻，所以他穿上粉色蓬蓬裙

“When Linda was diagnosed, that’s when I went to self-therapy mode. That’s what I do when things are getting hard. I start making photographs,” said Bob

“当Linda被确诊时，当事情开始变得困难起来的时候，我会进入自我治疗模式。我开始拍照，” Bob说


“It is about keeping everything light and not so serious,” claims Bob

“是要保持一切轻盈和不那么的严肃，” Bob说道


The endearing pictures of a middle-aged man wearing a pink tutu in many random places certainly made Linda laugh. But that wasn’t all. That was just the beautiful beginning

这些有着一个中年男人穿着粉色蓬蓬裙在各种随机地方的照片讨人喜欢，着实的让Linda笑了出来。但不仅如此，这只是一个美丽的开始

“The more I laugh, the better I feel,” Linda says

“我越笑，就感觉越来越好了，”Linda 说

The cancer treatment began and Linda would take the photos to show them to the other women

癌症治疗开始时，Linda会拿着这些照片给其他人看

The pictures went like hot cakes

这些照片火了起来

“Who would have thought a pink tutu would connect with so many people,” said Linda

”谁会想到一个粉色蓬蓬裙能连接起这么多人呢，“ Linda说

The pictures helped raise not only awareness about cancer, but also helped empower those in need of hope

这些照片不仅帮助了更多人了解癌症，但也给了这些需要希望的人能量
